Masala maggi

Description:

Ingredients:

  • Maggi two minute noodles-2 packs
  • Water-400ml
  • Gas/Induction stove
  • Cooking Vessel
  • Maggi masala(optional)

Steps for preparation:

  1. Add about 400ml of watter to the vessel
  2. Mount the vessel on the stove and heat it at a moderate temperature
  3. Open the noodle packets
