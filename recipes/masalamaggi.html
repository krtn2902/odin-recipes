Masala maggi

Description:

Indulge in the timeless delight of Classic Maggi Noodles, a beloved and iconic dish enjoyed by millions around the world. With its irresistible taste, quick preparation, and versatility, Maggi Noodles have become a go-to choice for a satisfying meal or snack. Whether you're a student in need of a quick bite or a busy professional seeking a delicious comfort food, this recipe will guide you through creating the perfect bowl of Maggi Noodles that will leave you craving for more.

Ingredients:

  • Maggi two minute noodles-2 packs
  • Water-400ml(4 cup)
  • Gas/Induction stove
  • Cooking Vessel
  • Maggi masala(optional)

Steps for preparation:

  1. Break the noodle cake into four pieces.
  2. Add the noodle cake, masala packet, and vegetables (if using) to a pot with 4 cups boiling water.
  3. Cook uncovered for 2 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  4. Do not drain the remaining water.
  5. Serve immediately.
